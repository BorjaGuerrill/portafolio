Our Services

At BPS Int (Borja Protection Services International), we are dedicated to providing top-notch security and protection services for individuals and businesses across the globe. Our team of highly-trained professionals is committed to ensuring the safety and security of our clients at all times. Here are some of the services we offer:

Executive Protection: Our executive protection services are tailored to the specific needs of high-profile individuals, celebrities, executives, and public figures. We provide discreet and proactive protection to ensure their safety wherever they go.


Event Security: From private parties to corporate events, our event security services are designed to provide a safe and secure environment for all attendees. Our team will assess the potential risks and develop a comprehensive security plan to mitigate any threats.


Security Consulting: Our security consulting services help businesses assess and strengthen their security measures. We provide expert advice on security protocols, risk assessments, crisis management, and more to help our clients protect their assets and personnel.


VIP Transport: Our VIP transport services offer safe and reliable transportation for high-profile individuals and executives. Our experienced drivers and security personnel ensure a smooth and secure journey from start to finish.


Residential Security: Our residential security services provide peace of mind for homeowners and residents. We offer 24/7 monitoring, access control, surveillance, and rapid response in case of emergencies to protect your property and loved ones.
